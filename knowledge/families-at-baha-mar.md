# Families at Baha Mar — Concierge Knowledge

*All facts verified against bahamar.com and operator sites, July 2026. Voice: warm-practical (Grand Hyatt register).*

## The essentials every family should know

Baha Bay, our luxury beachfront waterpark, is **included with your stay** — unlimited access for all resort guests, all three hotels. Twenty-four slides and attractions across 15 beachfront acres, with dedicated areas by age: Turtle Beach for toddlers, thrill slides for older kids, and the Flow Curl surf simulator. Private cabanas can be reserved for the day.

**Kids 5 and under eat free** at select restaurants across the resort. The nightly fountain show runs every 15 minutes in the evening — a favorite family gathering point, and free.

## The Explorers Club (kids club)

Supervised programming for children **ages 3–12** (potty-trained required). Themed three-hour sessions — cooking, crafts, wildlife visits, movie time — daily 9:00 AM–12:00 PM and 1:00 PM–4:00 PM, $65 per session. Rosewood guests also have their own dedicated Rosewood Explorers program.

Parents' tip the concierge should offer proactively: sessions pair beautifully with a Royal Blue tee time or an ESPA treatment — kids adventure while you unwind, everyone reunites for the fountain show.

## Wildlife at The Sanctuary

- **Turtle Tales** — a 20-minute hands-on encounter with our resident green sea turtles: touch, feed, and learn from the conservation team. **Minimum age 8; ages 8–12 must be accompanied by a paying adult.** Runs Thursday–Monday. *(This is a touch-and-feed encounter — guests do not swim with the turtles.)*
- **Flamingo Encounter** — meet our iconic Caribbean flamingo flock up close at Flamingo Cay, daily at 10:00 AM, 1:15 PM, and 4:15 PM; capacity is limited, so book ahead. The **flamingo parade at 9:30 AM is free** and unmissable with younger kids.
- The Sanctuary is also home to nurse sharks, macaws, and Aldabra tortoises.

## For the grown-ups

**Royal Blue** — The Bahamas' only Jack Nicklaus Signature golf course: dunes on the front nine, limestone landscapes and an island green on the back. Green fee $275, or the $325 all-in package (premium rental clubs, ProV1s, engraved bag tag). Tee times from 7:00 AM. Mini Blue, our 18-hole mini-golf, is the family version.

**ESPA** — the Caribbean's first ESPA: 30,000 square feet of spa inspired by The Bahamas. Signature 80-minute treatments from $295, daily 9:00 AM–7:00 PM.

## Dining with kids

Over 45 restaurants, bars, and lounges across the resort, from celebrity-chef dining (Marcus Samuelsson, Daniel Boulud, Katsuya) to casual poolside. Dinner reservations at signature restaurants sell out well ahead in peak season — the concierge should always offer to book early, and offer alternatives plus waitlisting when a first choice is full.
