# Celebrations & Groups — Concierge Knowledge

*All facts verified against slshotels.com, bahamar.com, and discotech, July 2026. Voice: upbeat, stylish, in on the fun (SLS register).*

## Why celebrate at Baha Mar

Milestone birthdays, bachelor and bachelorette parties, and group getaways are an SLS specialty — the playfully hip hotel at the heart of the resort's nightlife, steps from Baha Bay and the casino. Groups get the full campus: 45+ restaurants and bars, the Caribbean's largest casino, beach clubs, and three hotels of rooms to mix and match across budgets.

## The celebration circuit

- **Privilege Pool** — the adults-only pool club at SLS. Open Thursday–Sunday 11:00 AM–6:00 PM, with **DJs every Friday and Saturday**. Reserved seating carries minimum spends: lounge chairs from $300 (seats two), daybeds from $500, cabanas from $2,000 (up to 15 guests). A favorite for bachelorette parties — book the group's spot early for Saturday.
- **Bond** — the 10,000-square-foot nightclub inside SLS. **Friday and Saturday only, 11:00 PM–4:00 AM, 18+.** VIP tables with bottle service are reserved through SLS — smart dress code: collared shirts, no beachwear or athletic wear. The concierge reminds groups about the dress code when booking.
- **Monkey Bar & Skybar** — the warm-up and the view. **Katsuya** and **Fi'lia** handle the group dinner; large tables sell out weeks ahead in peak season — book the same day the dates are set.
- **Jazz Club by Jon Batiste** — nightly live shows; a more elevated group evening.
- Daytime: **Baha Bay cabanas** (park entry already included for guests), catamaran sunset cruises, ESPA group bookings, and the casino floor.

## Group reservations — how the concierge helps

Group stays (10+ guests, multiple rooms) are coordinated as one reservation with one confirmation number. The concierge can check availability for the full party, hold room blocks, move dates (see change policy), and coordinate the itinerary — dinner, pool club, nightlife — in one conversation. For 12 guests, always confirm: dates, headcount, room split, and the one special moment (a birthday cake at dinner, champagne on arrival, a photographer at the cabana).

## Event inquiries (RFI)

For weddings, milestone parties, and corporate events, the events team builds custom proposals. The concierge collects: **event type** (Wedding, Honeymoon, Bachelor/Bachelorette Party, Gender Reveal, Babymoon, Birthday, Bar/Bat Mitzvah, Anniversary), **preferred date** (approximate is fine), **headcount**, **preferred location** (Baha Mar-wide, Grand Hyatt, SLS, or Rosewood), and **contact details** — conversationally, one or two details at a time, saving as it goes so nothing is lost. A dedicated sales manager follows up within one business day.

Current celebration offers worth mentioning: Grand Hyatt's **"Celebrate More"** package ($350 in credits for birthdays, anniversaries, honeymoons, and babymoons — offer code CHEERS) and SLS's **"4th Night On Us"** with $250 F&B credit.

## Meetings & conventions

Roughly 200,000 square feet of flexible space, anchored by an 82,000-square-foot Convention, Arts & Entertainment Center — ballrooms seating up to 3,000, 14+ breakout rooms, plus beachfront and lawn venues. Corporate groups regularly book the resort years out; serious inquiries route to the events sales team.
