# Fees, Policies & Practical Answers — Concierge Knowledge

*All facts verified against hyatt.com, rosewoodhotels.com, and bahamar.com, July 2026. The concierge is proactively transparent about costs — surprise-free is spectacular.*

## Resort fees (differ by hotel — always answer per property)

- **Grand Hyatt Baha Mar:** $76.00 per room per day resort fee, plus a $21.78 per day service charge (tax included).
- **Rosewood Baha Mar:** resort fee of 16.5% of the room rate (includes 10% VAT).
- **SLS Baha Mar:** resort fee applies; confirm the current amount at booking.

What the resort fee covers: unlimited Baha Bay waterpark access, beach and pool amenities, fitness center, and resort-wide programming (including the nightly fountain show and daily flamingo parade).

Purchases on property carry VAT and an automatic service charge/gratuity — budget roughly 25% on top of menu prices. The concierge should say this plainly when guests ask about dining costs.

## Reservation changes (group-friendly policy)

- Date changes are **free up to 14 days before arrival**, subject to availability for the full party size.
- Within 14 days of arrival: one free change; further changes $150 per room.
- Changes for group reservations (multiple rooms) are handled as one request — the concierge confirms old dates, new dates, and guest count before committing, and reconfirms availability for the entire party.
- Cancellations are handled by phone with the hotel's reservations team — the concierge does not process cancellations.

## Payments

The concierge never takes payment details in chat. Experience bookings are charged to the room; anything requiring a card is completed at the front desk, on the phone with reservations, or through the secure booking engine.

## Getting here & getting around

Nassau's Lynden Pindling International Airport (NAS) is roughly 20 minutes from the resort — closer than Paradise Island. Within the resort, all amenities are shared across Grand Hyatt, SLS, and Rosewood: one campus, every restaurant, pool, and experience open to every guest.

## Weather promise

The **Hurricane Guarantee**: if a named storm affects travel, guests can rebook without penalty. Details at booking.
